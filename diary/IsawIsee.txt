As I sit staring at unwritten lines on a page, the sheer possibility it frightening. People have died over scribbles. It’s so interesting what sounds produced by vibrating flesh can do. They can guide one’s perception! They can tell you what to think! and it’s even more fascinating that paper stained by ink can start turning wheels of infinite thought. Now that we have a slate in our pocket, we don’t have to go to China to see the Dunhuang caves, nor to Egypt to hear the Nile. What a society it is that I exist in… as I capture light into frames and look over them, the past bubbles and the future fades. We never look at the sky. It’s so beautiful. I see everywhere, constant pursuit of wanting to capture the sky, or the first snowfall. Or proof of love, accomplishment. I should tell them to drop it and look at the sky. But then am I not participating in their game? Maybe I should enjoy the sky myself…. But how can I? It’s just so beautiful. Ahh.. if they could just drop everything for a second, they’d understand me.
